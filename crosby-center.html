Sidney Crosby - Offense Key Points

Create offense with the puck: Beat 1 opponent with a carry, or beat 2 opponents with a pass.

Create offense without the puck: Close support to puck carrier, find soft spot in high slot for quick release shot.

Sidney Crosby - Defense Key Points

Play like a 3rd defenseman: Staying D-side in OZ and NZ to prevent controlled exits and entries.

Low and slow on breakouts Get back early to help Dmen; provide an outlet in the middle of the ice.

Sidney Crosby - Puck Protection

Patience to take on pressure: Feel the pressure on your hip before making a move to beat defender

Small area skating Opportunity to implement heel-to-heel skating and mohawks
